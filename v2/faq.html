FAQs

WHAT IS SPARK CARE?
Spark Care is a way for Walmart Health & Wellness associates to recognize other associates’ Moments of Care – Safety, Kindness, Connection and Simplicity.

WHAT ARE THE FOUR MOMENTS OF CARE?
Safety must never be sacrificed. Everything must be safe. This encompasses personal safety, compliance and the preservation of personal health information.

Kindness reflects a desire to show compassion and empathy. Everyone should demonstrate kindness – even when they are busy.

Connection captures our desire to build a relationship with our customers and patients. Relationships can be made by the smallest gestures, like knowing a customer’s name.

Simplicity is a business need. Health care is complicated in the eyes of our customer. Our processes and communication should be simple to understand.
